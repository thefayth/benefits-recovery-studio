# Brand Style Notes

## Brand Position

Bold, intelligent, protective, human, practical, and receipt-driven.

## Visual Direction

- structured document flow;
- strong privacy boundary;
- warm accent colors;
- calm administrative clarity;
- human review gates;
- no generic SaaS dashboard wallpaper.

## Palette

- Ink: #101820
- Paper: #F7F3EA
- Signal Gold: #D9A441
- Civic Teal: #2A9D8F
- Coral Alert: #E76F51
- Deep Plum: #4B2E83

## Typography

Use strong, readable sans-serif typography with generous spacing and high contrast. Avoid decorative fonts for body copy.

## Voice

Direct, protective, strategic, and grounded in ownership.
