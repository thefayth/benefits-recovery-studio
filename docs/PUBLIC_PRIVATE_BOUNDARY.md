# Public / Private Boundary

## Public

- project name and purpose;
- high-level workflow;
- generated public-safe visuals;
- ownership notices;
- WordPress page draft;
- privacy review;
- commercial-use policy.

## Private

- medical files;
- benefits records;
- forms, notices, cards, packets, receipts, and portal sessions;
- private prompts and agent instructions;
- credentials, tokens, cookies, account details, and bank details;
- family information and identity records;
- operational infrastructure and source workflows.

## Human Gate

Any legal signature, filing, portal action, upload, call, or final submission remains human-controlled and outside this public repository.
