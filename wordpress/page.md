# Benefits Recovery Studio

![Benefits Recovery Studio hero](../assets/hero/hero-image.png)

## Hero

Public surface. Private engine. Receipts always.

Benefits Recovery Studio is Faith Cheltenham's protected public surface for a privacy-preserving administrative recovery workflow: organizing dense paperwork, deadlines, evidence, and follow-up loops without exposing private records.

## What This Project Is

A public-facing project page for document recovery, benefits paperwork organization, review gates, and receipt-ledger discipline.

## Why It Matters

People should not have to be at full capacity to survive paperwork systems. The work here turns scattered records and stressful deadlines into reviewable packets, clear status, and durable receipts.

## How It Works

1. Private records stay local and protected.
2. The workflow extracts only public-safe structure.
3. Review packets, answer banks, ledgers, and follow-up trackers organize the work.
4. Human legal actions stay gated.
5. The public surface explains the method without exposing the engine.
